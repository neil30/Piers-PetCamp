WHY WE CARE

Our camp exists to provide a shelter for adoptable companion animals without homes so that no one will endure the pain and suffering of life abandonment.

Homeless dogs and cats on the streets get hit by cars, are vulnerable to attacks by other animals and cruel people, and contract, spread, suffer and die from contagious diseases.

WHAT WE WANT

A heavenly community for our animal companions where people can found new friends that they can depend on to build a safe haven for each other.

After all, for man's best friend, no medal could ever replace the comfort of home and the love of family.

You Can Make a Difference

Help pets in need through sheltering, adoption, education, spay/neuter, and community outreach.

Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS)

PAWS drafted Republic Act 8485 or the Philippine Animal Welfare Bill. It promotes adopting instead of buying pets in the Philippines, which increased the number who actually did.

Visit Website

Philippine Animal Rescue Team (PART)

PART has a four-hectare farm in San Jose del Monte City, Bulacan, where 500 homeless dogs, cats, turtles, chickens, turkeys and goats roam freely.

Visit Website

Animal Kingdom Foundation, Inc. (AKF)

AKF has rescued thousands of slaughter-bound dogs and jailed numerous dog traders. Together with the Philippine National Police, they raid slaughterhouses and establishments where dog trading and meat selling happen.
